## 表关系图

```
users ──┬── user_roles ── roles
       ├── departments (manager)
       ├── projects (project_manager)
       ├── approvals (approver)
       └── audit_logs (user)

projects ──┬── contracts
           ├── purchase_lists ── purchase_list_items
           ├── stock_in ── stock_in_items
           ├── stock_out ── stock_out_items
           ├── construction_milestones
           ├── construction_progress
           └── completion_drawings

contracts ──┬── suppliers
            ├── batch_purchase_orders
            ├── income_statements
            └── material_payments

inventory ──┬── inventory_logs
            ├── stock_in_items
            └── stock_out_items
```
